We Buy Gold, Silver and Platinum Bullion

We buy all types of bullion in Manhattan, New York. Bullion refers to a precious metal in bulk form that is valued by how much it weighs, in either troy ounce or grams. Bullion can come in forms such as coins, bars, ingots, and rounds. It also comes in gold, silver, platinum, and palladium.

Bars: A bar denotes the shape of cast metal, marked with weight and purity for transparency. Valued in gold, silver, platinum, or palladium, bars are sought after by investors worldwide.

Ingots: Primarily referencing the shape of cast metal, this term applies to both precious and non-precious metals.

Rounds: A bar denotes the shape of cast metal, marked with weight and purity for transparency. Valued in gold, silver, platinum, or palladium, bars are sought after by investors worldwide.

Coins: Bullion coins are designed as investment pieces and are not intended for circulation. Often, the intrinsic value of the precious metal in these coins exceeds their face value.
